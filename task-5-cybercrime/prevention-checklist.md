# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
## General Safety Tips
1. Always check the sender’s email address before trusting any message.
2. Do not click on suspicious or unknown links received via email or SMS.
3. Never share sensitive information like passwords, OTPs, or personal details online.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
6. Keep your device software and apps updated to avoid security risks.
7. Verify internship offers, job opportunities, or messages through official websites.
8. Avoid downloading files or attachments from unknown sources.
 ## UPI / Financial Safety Tips (IMPORTANT)
1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone under any circumstances.
2. Do not approve payment requests from unknown persons on UPI apps.
3.Double-check payment details before sending money online.
4. Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources.
## Reporting Cybercrime (MANDATORY)
1. Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
2. Cybercrime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
