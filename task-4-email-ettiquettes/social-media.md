# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## DO’s
•	Maintain a professional and respectful profile 
•	Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts 
•	Think carefully before posting or sharing content 
•	Verify information before forwarding or reposting 
•	Be polite and respectful in comments and messages 
------------

## DON’Ts
•	Do not share personal or sensitive information (phone number, OTP, passwords) 
•	Do not post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content 
•	Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages 
•	Do not spread fake news or unverified information 
•	Do not engage in cyberbullying or negative online behavior
------------
